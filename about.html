Background

Anxiety and Behavioral Program for Children

For 25+ years, I ran a program called the Anxiety and Behavioral Program for Children, with offices in Bala Cynwyd, Devon, and Paoli. The program focused on young children with developmental disabilities, physical disabilities, and significant chronic health problems. I focused on the treatment of anxiety and behavior problems in children, helping them learn better ways to manage their struggles. I have worked with children as young as 2 years old to help parents find ways to solve problems their children are facing.

Current Work

In YEAR, I decided to go back into clinical psychology. I missed working with clients and decided to set up a practice in Paoli. I also have a small home office in South Philadelphia suitable only for individual adult clients. I am continuing my work with children and adults, helping them overcome and cope with trauma and anxiety based behaviors.

Early Work

Early in my carrer, I completed a clinical psychology internship at Central Montgomery Mental Health Center in Norristown. I worked with developmentally delayed preschoolers, doing developmental evaluations and consulting with parents and teachers for five years in Northeast Philadelphia. I was also the Director of Psychology at Children's Rehabilitation Hospital in Philadelphia for four years.

Experience Abroad

I took an early retirement for 15 years to do international volunteering in East Africa and teaching in China. I was also a seasonal National Park Ranger for 10 summer seasons in Haleakala National Park in Hawaii and Yellowstone National Park in Wyoming.

Education

I have my PhD. in Clinical Psycology from Temple University. I also graduated from Bryn College with a degree in philosophy.
