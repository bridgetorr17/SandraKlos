Sandra's Background

Anxiety and Behavioral Program for Children

For 25+ years, Dr. Klos ran a program called the Anxiety and Behavioral Program for Children, with offices in Bala Cynwyd and the Devon to Paoli area. Her early work experience focused on young children with developmental disabilities, physical disabilities, and significant chronic health problems. She focuses on the treatment of anxiety and behavior problems in children, helping them learn better ways to manage their struggles. Dr. Klos has worked with chilren as young as 2 years old to help parents find ways to solve problems their children are facing.
